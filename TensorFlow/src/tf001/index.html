Welcome to Hoomanator's Live Object Recognizer!

This AI tool identifies objects in a live video as you are moving the camera using the machine learning MobileNet model.

It's very easy to use. You can use it on any platform. Once you are prompted to use the camera, simply allow the camera to be used (just one time) on your phone, desktop, laptop, etc., and check out the labels ("objects") with their corresponding confidence levels at the bottom of the screen. Your video will not be saved anywhere in our servers or cloud. Everything runs on your device. Totally safe & free!
