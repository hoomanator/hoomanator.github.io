Welcome to Hoomanator's HandPose Model!

This AI tool applies the HandPose model using ml5.js. You can track both hand keypoints and represent each keypoint with a different color for different hands.

It's very easy to use. You can use it on any platform. Once you are prompted to use the camera, simply allow the camera to be used (just one time) on your phone, desktop, laptop, etc. Your video will not be saved anywhere in our servers or cloud. Everything runs on your device. Totally safe & free!
