Welcome to Hoomanator's Image Recognizer!

This AI tool identifies objects in your image using machine learning.

It's very easy to use. You can use it on any platform. Simply press "Choose file" on your phone, desktop, laptop, etc., select a photo or video from your photo library or your computer, and check out the labels("objects") with their corresponding confidence levels at the bottom of the image. Your image will not be saved anywhere in our servers or cloud. Everything runs on your device. Totally safe & free!
